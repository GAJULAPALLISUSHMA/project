JAGANANNA VIDYADEEVENA

The State Government of Andhra Pradesh Started a new welfare scheme for ITI, B.Tech, B. Pharmacy, MBA, MCA, and B.Ed students. The scheme named “YSR Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme“. The scheme launched on 27 November 2019. Vidya Deevena Scheme launched by Andhra Pradesh CM YS Jagan Mohan Reddy. Candidates can read complete details regarding Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme 2019 AP can go through this article. The scheme provides Fee Reimbursement for ITI, B.Tech, B. Pharmacy, MBA, MCA, and B.Ed Courses. Selected students get an amount of Rs. 15000 to 20000 under YSR Vidya Deevena Scheme 2019-20.

YSR Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme in AP – Details

Scheme Will Be Supervised By Andhra Pradesh Education Department Name Of The Scheme Jagananna Vidya Deevena Implemented By CM YS Jagan Mohan Reddy Approval Of Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme 27th November 2019 Implementation Of This Scheme 24th February 2020 Category Govt Schemes Offered Pay ITI students will receive Rs 10,000, polytechnic students Rs 15,000, and degree students Rs 20,000 every year Scheme Purpose To Provide 100% fee reimbursement for SC, ST, OBC, minority, Kapus, EWS, disabled Mode Of Application Form Online Scheme Launched In Andhra Pradesh

Documents Required to apply for Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme

*Aadhar card
*Residential Proof
*Income certificate
*BPL or EWS certificate
*college admission certificate
*Admission fee receipt
*Non-tax payer declaration
*Parent's occupational certificate
*Bank account details
